Totton Santa Run

9th December, Empire Hall Totton, Totton

Only: Child £6, Adult £12, or Family £32 (two adults / two children) Register now!

About

A family friendly, Christmas fun run, this year with an indoor Fayre at the finish line for runners and spectator enjoyment.

Please join us in Totton's third Santa Run, a 2.5km dask around Totton dressed in Santa suits or other Yuletide Garb, complete with decorated pushchair and festive dog competition

There is also the Xmas Fayre, for finishline frolics complete with Reindeer, Cake competition, Chior, Raffle and more...

Location

This year we are changing the route to a 2.5km circular route starting and finishing at Library Road in the center of Totton, This allows spectators to see both the begining and end of the run and we hope will allow drivers slightly shorter period of disruption on Salisbury Road.

  1. Start - Library Road
  2. Turn right up Salisbury Road
  3. Turn right onto Testwood Lane (At the Testwood club junction)
  4. Run the entire length of Testwood Lane turning on to right Library Road by the Police Station
  5. Finish - Near the Library

As with other years we will clear the route as soon as possible opening the roads as soon as it is safe to do so.
