About

Please join us in Totton's fourth Santa Run, a 2.5km dash around Totton dressed in Santa suits or other Yuletide garb, complete with decorated pushchair and festive dog competition

There is also the Xmas Fayre, for finish line frolics complete with Choir, Raffle and more...

Location

This year's 2.5km circular route starts and finishs at Library Road in the centre of Totton, This allows spectators to see both the beginning and end of the run.
